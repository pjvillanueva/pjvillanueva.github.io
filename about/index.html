About Adventist Explorers App

Our Story

Adventist Explorers App is a Bible study guide application dedicated to advocating and teaching Seventh-day Adventist (SDA) beliefs. While our app is inspired by SDA teachings, it is important to note that we are not officially affiliated with the Seventh-day Adventist Church.

The app was developed by New Town Productions, a startup based in the Philippines. Our mission is to spread the Word of God and reach more people through modern mediums such as mobile applications.

Mission & Purpose

Our mission is to create accessible, reliable, and inspiring Bible study materials that empower individuals to deepen their faith. We believe that technology can be a powerful tool to connect people with the message of the Bible and the teachings of the Seventh-day Adventist faith.
